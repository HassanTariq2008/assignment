The Story

The film follows two friends, GOKU and VIGITA, Dragon Ball Z is a legendary Japanese anime series created by Akira Toriyama and produced by Toei Animation. It follows the adult life of Son Goku as he defends Earth from powerful foes like aliens, androids, and magical beings. With intense battles, iconic transformations like Super Saiyan, and themes of friendship, perseverance, and redemption, the show became a global phenomenon. Originally airing from 1989 to 1996, it spawned sequels, movies, and a massive fanbase that continues to thrive today.
